Data & AI strategy · the data contract

What a data contract contains.

A producer-signed commitment — what they publish, with what quality, under what governance. From today's schema-only file to the governed five-clause target. Aligned with the Open Data Contract Standard v3.1.0 ↗.

02 Full data contract — template Target
The five clauses every producer signs — committing not just to schema, but to access, compliance, and quality. One contract can reference many schemas, each feeding a data product. Same template whether data flows through the pipeline or an MCP.

A contract may reference many schemas. Each carries a type — Source (raw feed), Master (authoritative), or Composed (derived) — plus its own Domain, target data product(s), and intended uses.
