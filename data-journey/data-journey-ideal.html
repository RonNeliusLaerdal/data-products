What that gets us
It holds

Nothing breaks silently.

When the producer changes something, the contract catches it — consumers are warned, and what depends on the data keeps working instead of shattering unnoticed.

It creates value

The data becomes reusable.

Because it's found, trusted, and reusable, the insights, decisions, and AI products that were out of reach become routine — the value stops being left on the table.
