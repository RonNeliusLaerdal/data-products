Data & AI strategy · scaling the model

From single products to a shared domain.

Each producer's data is already a governed product. When several of them need one shared concept, a cross-product domain owns the canonical definition they all follow.

01 A governed single product
Building on the source-aligned data journey: one producer publishes its own data with a contract, schema, and owner — governed end to end.

03 A cross-product domain, governed
A Domain Owner — Engagement & Usage — owns the canonical Session schema. Each producer adds it to their contract alongside their own product signals, so three pipelines converge into one trusted Session product that serves every consumer, human or AI.
